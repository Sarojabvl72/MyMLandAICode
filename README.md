Title: Coupon Acceptance Rate Analysis

Summary:  Overall coupon acceptance rate was 56.84% (n = 12,684). Acceptance varies substantially by coupon type: Carry out & Take away and Restaurant (<$20) show the highest acceptance (73.55% and 70.92% respectively), while Bar and Restaurant ($20–$50) show the lowest acceptance (41.00% and 44.10% respectively). The association between coupon type and time of day is highly significant (p ≈ 1.50×10⁻²²⁴).
